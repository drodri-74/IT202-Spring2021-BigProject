This is an application where it shows you the available departments for sale from the data of the "Cityofchicago.org"

Explanation:

You will have to click on the menu(top-left corner of the page) and then click on the "Search" option. Then you could search affordable rent in chicago based on the three options listed.

Once you are done picking all of your three options(Community name, Property type, Units), click on search and then go to the menu tab and search for the "Map" option. It will show you the locations of the results from "Search" option.

Lastly the "About" Option shows you data used in the application and some other information.
